The Framework

Four disciplines.
One conviction.

Each technical analysis framework gives a distinct view of market movement — structure, cycle, execution, momentum. Together they remove ambiguity and produce clarity: a unified read of what the market is doing and a concrete action plan.

01

Wyckoff

Institutional accumulation and distribution leave a footprint in volume and price spread. Wyckoff decodes that footprint — mapping the structural phases that precede major moves long before they reach the breakout bar. It separates reading price from understanding what's actually driving it.

02

Elliott Wave Analysis

Markets move in identifiable cycles. Elliott Wave provides the structural roadmap — locating where price stands in the larger pattern and projecting high-probability targets before the move fully develops. Every trade has a thesis, not just a trigger.

The cleanest signal is still the chart. Candlestick behavior at key levels, institutional order flow fingerprints, structural reactions — read without lag or distortion. Fewer tools, sharper reads. Clarity itself is an edge.
